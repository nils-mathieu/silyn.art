I remember discovering Glacier while autoplaying Monstercat releases on YouTube. I was immediately
struck by the quality of the mix he was managing. His music has so many elements working with each
other in (literal) harmony to create one of the most complex yet beautiful soundscape I can think
of.

His style is pretty close to what I'm trying to get to with my own music, so he has been one of my
biggest inspiration. It's a mix of what we would call today Botanica or Petalcore, but maybe in a
bit more mainstream way.

I definitely still reference [a](https://youtu.be/YEvRz7Pw8aI) [lot](https://youtu.be/d-1A596jORw)
[of](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aY0I-q1Z-y0) [his](https://youtu.be/jMB5mwPOU60)
[tracks](https://youtu.be/qcE__zJ4L6E?list=OLAK5uy_lUBbRb5_EJL3w4BGUuP_Bj9CYcxXI8Y1I) when I write
my own music, and I'm more generally coming back to his music regularly. I literally cannot get
tired of it. And god knows how fast I can get bored of the music I listen to too often.
