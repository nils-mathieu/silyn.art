I remember discovering Exyl the day he released his first [Ping!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RKW6rjnYEkc),
about when it was going viral on YouTube. That funny clip I shared to my friends back then turned
out to be the rise of what would be eventually one of my favorite artists. The time he spends on
both his music and the visuals that accompany hit is something that I deeply respect.

Pretty sure his music is meant to be something like Complextro but I don't really consider it
as so anymore. He's literally grown out of the genre, following what is pretty much a style on its
own.

Exyl also holds a spcial place in my heart because it's his music that motivated me to get back
into music production in early 2023 after almost two years of not touching my DAW.
