Camellia is, if I remember correctly, the first big realization I had in music. It was the first
time I thought: "yo WTF this is so creative!". I'm pretty sure I started listening to his music with
his EP [Cyphisonia](https://cametek.bandcamp.com/album/cyphisonia-e-p) and it's been a wild ride
ever since!

That guy has such a unique style you can recognize him even
[on random BGM tracks](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H8_LUZGNA2c) he made without checking for
the credits.

Whether it's crazy full-flavor electronic drops or impactful and effective melodies, if you like
ryhthm game music and are ready to let yourself be surprised by his impredictible drops, then do
yourself a favor and go listen to his entire discography. Even his older stuff is
[absolutely wild](https://youtu.be/NTMp2flMxQs?feature=shared&t=162) and (quite literally) genre
defining!
