This track has a little bit of an history. It was made in about two days in reaction to me
discovering last minute the [Cosmic Radio 2024] contest created by [Pero Pero Games] (the guys
behind [Muse Dash]) in collaboration with a few rythm game companies.

Normally, the contest is supposed to span over two months. From February to April. But because I'm
a bit of an idiot, I started working on it in April, on the 27th,. The deadline was on the 29th.
So, yeah. After two white nights, I was able to submit my song to the contest.

A few months later, Pero Pero Games ran a live stream on Youtube to anounce the winners. And I
was among them! I was awarded with the 5th prize, along with 22 other amazing musicians. I must
confess that seeing my name next to some of the people I listen regularly and look up to like
BilliumMoto or MEMODEMO felt fantastic. Along with a tinge of impostor syndrom though ;).

Thanks again Pero Pero Games for making this contest. I got to discover a whole lot of great
artists while listening to other entries.
