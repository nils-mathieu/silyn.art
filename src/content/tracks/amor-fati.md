> I want to learn more and more to see as beautiful what is necessary in things;
> then I shall be one of those who makes things beautiful. Amor fati: let that be my
> love henceforth! I do not want to wage war against what is ugly. I do not want to accuse;
> I do not even want to accuse those who accuse. Looking away shall be my only negation. And
> all in all and on the whole: some day I wish to be only a Yes-sayer.
*- Nietzsche's The Gay Science*

This piece was created for the AIM2025 (Art Inspired Music) contest on Newgrounds. The idea
was to create a music track from an image we liked. I chose that one:

![Girl looking into the distance](https://img.ngfiles.com/image-uploads/000/001/411/iu_1411770_18882320.webp)

What really stroke me when stumbling on Miloecute's art was the vibe of the scene she had managed
to set up. The luminous night sky, the wind, the plains, the wide horizon -- every is set up in way
that I couldn't help but admire. This vibe drove the instrumental part of the song which uses
acoustic instruments extensively (piano, acoustic guitar, violin, marimba).

And perhaps more importantly, I wondered what the girl was thinking. What was she looking at? That
part inspired the lyrics of the song. I figured being in the middle of such a wonderful scenery 
would make you realise how beautiful the world is and how important it is to preserve its beauty.
And by beauty, I mean both the literal physical beauty, and the
people-had-to-die-for-this-place-to-exist kind of beauty.

Anyways, it was fun working on this.
