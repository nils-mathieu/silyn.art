I remember when Miro Calliope first debuted as a v-tuber in the Hololive English group. I
didn't immediately realize who she was until she started singing. Then I knew I had already
heard her before. She had accompanied me during the end of my highschool days, and my time in
Classe Préparatoire, when I was studying for my entrance exams to engineering schools (to which I
never actually ended up going to). I must say, I found it a bit funny to find her under this new
identity.

And new identity, in that case, also meant new music. I was already quite fond of her music before,
but with bigger resources and access to a wider range of producers, her music just
got to a whole new level. Somewhere between doujin rap music from Japan and DTM high quality
productions, her music is a mix of a little bit of everything I like. She really learnt to
find a style of her own, and each release seems to get better than the last.

Now she even signed with Universal, which is a big deal for the independent undergroud artist
she was a few years back. I hope she can make this kind of music more mainstream. I'm really happy
for her and I hope she keeps on making music for a long time.
