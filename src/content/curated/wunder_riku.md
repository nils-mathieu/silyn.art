Riku has been one of my inspirations since I discovered him in 2023. That guy is
*versatile* I tell you. From fast and joyful J-Pop, to chill beats, to aggressive trap, to EDM, there
is no single genre to fit that guy in.

And it's not just vibes! His résumé is genuinely staggering. He's a platinum-selling songwriter and
producer with Oricon & Billboard Japan #1 hits to his name. He wrote "1999" for King & Prince
(Universal Music) and "Unstoppable" for Kid Phenomenon (LDH/Sony Music Publishing). These are
household names in Japan. The dude is casually operating at the very top of the mainstream J-Pop world
while still deeply rooted in the VTuber and anime community.

When I think of where I want to be skill-wise and carrier-wise if I'm lucky and good enough at some point,
becoming that guy is my target.
