This song was originally made in around 16 hours at the occasion of the any%: Level 2 event
organized by FORM.

I always like to participate in their events because it gives me an excuse to leave my
usual style a bit and just do whatever I want for a short amount of time.

This time I decided to try and write an J-rock (kinda) song for a pretend space opera anime
about illegal races. Like imagine something like REDLINE or Initial D, but with space ships
and with races spanning entire star systems!

At the end of the 16 hours, I was pretty proud of what I had and submitted the song. A couple days
later, my song was selected to be the final event album!

You can listen to it [here on Soundcloud][original-soundcloud]
for free. But if you can, consider [buying the album or individual songs][any-percent-level-2]
on Bandcamp. All proceeds from any%: Level 2 will to be donated to [CHADS](www.chadscoalition.org/),
a nonprofit dedicated to awareness, prevention, and programs surrounding depression and suicide.
