I don't really remember when I started listening to Elliot Hsu's music. Pretty sure I just randomly
poped up on my Spotify feed. I was immediately transported to a world of dreamy, fantasy-like
soundscapes. Most of his music is orchestral and japanese, something you'd hear in an JRPG,
an advanture anime.

In each of his songs, you can *feel* the story behind it. They take you by the hand and
guide you through a world of magic and wonder. I can't help but feel inspired when I listen to his
music, and I repeatedly find myself referencing his songs and chord progressions in my own work.

A few of you know I'm working on a video game. Elliot's music is basically my main inspiration
for the game's soundtrack. One day I hope to be able to work with them on that.
