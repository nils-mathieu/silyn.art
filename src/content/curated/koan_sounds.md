These absolute mad-lads of sound design are always back where I least expect them with even crazier
stuff and ideas.

Originally, I was mainly listening to their music for their crazy reese bass design driving the
unique vibe of their music. It's probably derived somehow of Neurofunk or Drum and Bass, but their
take is way more chill and technical than what I was used to hear. Listen to
[any of their older tracks](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=457mf5wWovk), you'll get what I mean.
Even older than that, they were featured in [this legendary collab](https://youtu.be/NHqxducdkcc).
That shit is 12 years old and the mix is absolutely pristine. Do you remember what EDM sounded like
in 2012? Not like that that's for sure.

As of lately, they seem to have gone for a more narrative approach to music and sound design. They
surprised me by making me enjoy and look forward to what is effectively the album equivalent of an
art film.
